Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the savings calculator?

Our calculator uses real-time fuel prices and average EV efficiency data to provide estimates within 5-10% accuracy. Actual savings may vary based on driving habits and electricity rates.

Are EVs really better for the environment?

Yes! Even when accounting for electricity generation, EVs produce 50-70% fewer emissions than petrol/diesel cars over their lifetime, and Rajasthan's growing solar energy makes them even cleaner.

How long does charging take at your stations?

Our fast chargers (50kW) can charge most EVs to 80% in 30-45 minutes. Standard chargers (7kW) take 4-6 hours for a full charge.

Do I need a special membership to charge?

No membership required! You can pay per session via our app, UPI, or contactless cards. Members get 10% discount though.

What's the maintenance cost difference?

EVs save ₹15,000-20,000 annually as they don't need oil changes, have fewer moving parts, and regenerative braking reduces wear on brakes.

Are there government incentives for EVs?

Yes! Rajasthan offers:
- ₹10,000/kWh battery subsidy (max ₹30,000)
- 100% road tax exemption
- Free registration for 3 years
