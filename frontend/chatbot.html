FAQ

How to book?
Type "book <station> on <date> at <time>" or use the Booking page.
Why can't I see prices?
Some stations don't publish pricing. Provide the station name and I'll try to fetch it.
Is my car supported?
Ask "connector at <station>" with your car model to check common compatibility.
