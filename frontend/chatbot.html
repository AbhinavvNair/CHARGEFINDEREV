Frequently Asked

How do I book a slot?
Type "book <station> on <date> at <time>" or visit the Booking page for a visual interface.
Why can't I see prices?
Some stations don't publish pricing publicly. Ask me about a specific station and I'll try to fetch it for you.
Is my vehicle supported?
Ask "connector at <station> for <vehicle>" to check compatibility with your car, bike, or scooter.
Can I find nearby stations?
Yes! Type "nearby" or "near me" and allow location access to find the closest charging stations.
